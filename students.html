Student Experience

Discover what it's like to be part of Tufts Consulting Collective. From collaborative project work to professional development, our analysts gain hands-on experience in management consulting while building lasting relationships.

A Week in the Life of a TCC Analyst

Follow along as we take you through a typical week in the semester, showcasing the variety of experiences and learning opportunities our analysts enjoy.

SUN

Weekly Team Meeting

2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Meet with your project team to present the previous week's deliverables and discuss findings. Collaborate to outline tasks and priorities for the upcoming week, ensuring everyone is aligned on objectives and deadlines.

MON

Weekly Deliverable Deep Dive

7:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Begin working on this week's deliverables with focused research and analysis. Dive into your assigned workstream, conduct market research, analyze data, and start developing insights that will contribute to the team's overall project goals.

TUE

TCC Run Club

8:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Join TCC's run club to connect with fellow members outside of project work. Build relationships, stay active, and engage in casual conversations that strengthen the community bond.

WED

Professional Development Lecture

6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Attend TCC's professional development lecture series featuring industry experts, alumni from top consulting firms, and senior members sharing insights on consulting frameworks, case study methodologies, and career development.

THU

Partner Collaboration Session

4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Meet with a teammate to collaborate on this week's deliverables. Work together to synthesize research, and develop recommendations while learning from each other's analytical approaches.

FRI

Social Events

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Unwind with fellow TCC members through a variety of bonding activities. These social events foster team spirit, create lasting friendships, and provide a fun way to decompress after a productive week.

SAT

Finish off Weeks Deliverables

3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Finalize the week's work and create a slide presentation for the weekly team meeting. Synthesize findings, prepare talking points, and ensure all deliverables are polished and ready for presentation.
