Why TCC?

CHANGE THIS: Tufts Consulting Collective combines rigorous student talent with professional-grade consulting frameworks to deliver meaningful, cost-effective solutions. Whether you're a non-profit, startup, or established company, TCC provides expert analysis, strategic recommendations, and actionable insights tailored to your unique challenges.

Practice Areas

Market Analysis

Trend-tracking and in-depth analysis of different demographics to provide targeted go-to-market and expansion strategies

Partnerships Analysis

Extensive research about prospective partnering organizations to provide recommendations about potential conjunctures for clients

Pricing Strategy

Comparisons of client's product prices with those of competitors' and analysis of buyer & seller power

Economics Impact

Quantitative assessment of revenue potential, cost savings, and broader industry effects to evaluate financial viability and long-term value

Go to Market Strategy

Development of tailored launch, distribution, and customer acquisition plans to maximize adoption and sustainable growth

Competitive Analysis

Benchmarking of industry players, market share, strengths, and weaknesses to identify differentiation opportunities and strategic positioning

How It Works

01

Project Team Staffing

We pair your company with a 9-person team of Tufts' passionate students lead by 2 Project Managers to help you solve a critical problem.

02

Assessing Our Value Creation

We begin by asking our client what their main pain points are and how/where we can step in via individualized project work streams.

03

Pre-Semester Planning

We schedule client calls before the semester to scope out the overarching business problem and draft out a plan of action of how to tackle the problem throughout the semester.

04

Semester-long Research

Our PMs and analysts work throughout the semester to perform research and put together a final Assertion Statement and Presentation.

05

Mid-semester Presentations

We present our preliminary findings and insights to the client mid-way through the semester, ensuring alignment and incorporating feedback to refine our approach and keep clients involved at all stages.

06

Final Deliverables

Our project teams will present our final deliverable and recommendations to the client at the end of the semester.
